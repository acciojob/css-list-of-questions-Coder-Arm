1. Who was the first female to become the governor of an Indian state?

Sarojini Naidu was the first woman to become the governor of an Indian state.

2. Who among first Woman to climb Mount Everest twice?

Santosh Yadav (born 10 October 1967) is an Indian mountaineer. She is the first woman in the world to climb Mount Everest twice and the first woman to successfully climb Mount Everest from Kangshung Face.

3. Who is President of India?

Draupadi Murmu
